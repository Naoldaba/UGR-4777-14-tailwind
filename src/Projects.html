Project Overview

Abronet.org is a specific Fintech platform that aims to modernize and digitize the traditional Equb concept, providing a digital platform for individuals to participate in similar group savings and lending activities.

An Equb, also known as an Iqub or Hundi, is a traditional rotating savings and credit association found in various cultures around the world. In an Equb, a group of individuals contribute a fixed amount of money at regular intervals (e.g., weekly or monthly).

The total sum is then given to one member of the group in a cycle, and this process repeats until all members have received their share. Equbs provide a way for people to save and access capital in communities where formal banking services may be limited.

Project Overview

ESSTI.org is a website that serves as online platform for the Ethiopian space science and technology institute.The Ethiopian Space Science Society (ESSTI) is an organization dedicated to advancing space science and technology in Ethiopia. It aims to promote the study and exploration of space-related sciences, as well as to develop local expertise and capacity in this field.

This website plays a crucial role in forstering interest and education in space sciences, and it may also be involved in collaborative projects, research, and initiatives related to space exploration, satellite technology, and other aerospace endeavors.

This web platfom will extensively used for information dissemination by providing information about ESSTI's mission,vision, objectives, and activities related to space science and technology in ethiopia. It also helps to offer Educational Resourses, Promotion of space science, to provide project updates.

Project Overview

ETwood is an Ethiopian Movie streaming site/ online platform that allow users to watch movies and TV shows over the internet. This site have become immensely popular due to its convenience it offer in accessing a wide range of content without the need for physical media or traditional television broadcasting. Here are some of the key uses and features of ETwood

1. On-Demand Viewing
Users can watch movies and TV shows at their own convenience, without being bound by a specific broadcast schedule.
2. Vast Content Library
This site typically offer a diverse catalog of movies, TV series, documentaries, and other content spanning various genres, languages, and time periods.
3. Content Categorization
Content is usually organized into categories or genres, making it easy for users to find movies or shows that match their interests.
4. User Reviews and Ratings
Users can often rate and review movies and TV shows, providing valuable feedback to other viewers and helping them make informed choices.
5. Original Content
The platform has its own exclusive content ,known as "originals," which can only be viewed on their platform.
